This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

Last week's bootcamp at the University of Toronto was not the most successful one we've ever done: quite a few registrants didn't show up, and based on feedback from the instructors, we tried to cover too much, too fast. That said, the learners who stuck with us all the way through to Friday afternoon came away knowing a lot of useful things (or at least knowing that those things existed). We'll gear back a bit for next week's workshop at Indiana U. and see how that goes.
