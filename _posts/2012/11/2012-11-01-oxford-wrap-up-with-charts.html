This post originally appeared on the [Software Carpentry website](https://software-carpentry.org/)

The first Software Carpentry workshop in Oxford has wrapped up, and by all accounts was a big success. Philip Fowler, the host, has posted about running the workshop ("everyone sat up a bit straighter during version control...") and also done an analysis of what participants think they learned. In addition, Aron Ahmadia has given us some detailed feedback on how we could improve things the next time 'round. Our thanks to everyone involved—we're looking forward to a return visit soon.
