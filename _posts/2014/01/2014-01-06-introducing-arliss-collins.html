The incredible expansion of Software Carpentry over the last few months has been exciting and a little overwhelming, so we're pleased to introduce the newest member of the Mozilla Science Lab team, Arliss Collins. Arliss's background is in engineering and project management, and she'll be working with me, Kaitlin and Greg on bootcamp management, infrastructure development and maintenance, and communications.
