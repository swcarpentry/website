This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

Software Carpentry values the participation of every member of the scientific community and want all attendees to have an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. Thanks to Pauline Barmby, we now have an accessibility checklist for instructors and workshop hosts. Please have a look and send us fixes and suggestions for improvements.
