The Center for High Throughput Computing (CHTC) at the University of Wisconsin-Madison provides compute resources and services to campus researchers at UW-Madison, and also develops the HTCondor computational scheduling software, which is used all over the world. We are seeking to hire a Systems Integration Developer, who will help us to accelerate the work of researchers through computational middleware scripting and the design of workflow tools. Please see the online posting at http://www.ohr.wisc.edu/WebListing/Unclassified/PVLSummary.aspx?pvl_num=78987 for details.
