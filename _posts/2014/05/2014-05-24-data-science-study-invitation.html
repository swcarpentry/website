Katie Kuksenok, a graduate student at the University of Washington, is interviewing academic researchers who do data science to explore the barriers and challenges they face. If you would be willing to take 20-30 minutes to be interviewed, please get in touch: among other things, her findings will be used to help improve curriculum for projects like Software Carpentry.
