This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

The Mozilla Science Lab is looking for a community manager to build and scale existing community outreach efforts, and for a developer to lead technical prototyping efforts and engage with our community about technical projects. Possible office locations for these positions include Brooklyn, Toronto, London, Vancouver, and San Francisco, but we will consider remote working opportunities for the right candidate. For more information, or to apply, please see these postings.
