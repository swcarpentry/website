This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

Nature has just published a short interview about Software Carpentry with Kaitlin Thaney, the director of the Mozilla Science Lab, and two bootcamp attendees (Harvard's Rebecca Perry and USNIDA's Brian Sadacca). As always, version control seems to have been one of their big take-aways, but as Kaitlin says, two days isn't enough for people to become proficient: our bootcamps really are just the first step.
