Two announcements from the Software Sustainability Institute caught our eye this week:

  • Their next Collaborations Workshop will be held on March 26-27, 2014, in Oxford. This annual gathering brings together researchers, software developers, managers, funders and more to explore important ideas in software and research and to plant the seeds of interdisciplinary collaborations.
  • Sixteen new SSI Fellows have been selected: congratulations to them all!
