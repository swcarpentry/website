This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

The Mentoring Subcommittee is seeking for new members to help with the debriefing sessions and future activities. If you are interested in join us, please send a email to mentoring@lists.software-carpentry.org.

Currently members of the Mentoring Subcommittee spend around two hours each week with subcommittee activities that includes

  • attend two subcommittee meetings of one hour per month,
  • host two debriefing sessions of one hour per month,
  • write blog post about the debriefing sessions for the community, and
  • one-to-one call with others instructors upon request.

New members of the Mentoring Subcommittee will help currently members in the activities mention above and engage in future activities (more on that in the upcoming months).

This is a great opportunity to meet members of our community and help it grow. We hope you'll want to take part.
