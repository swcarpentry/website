Research Software Engineer position available at the Oxford e-Research Centre, University of Oxford, UK (Java, Python, Web-app, Database, Semantic-web).

In this varied, interesting and challenging role the successful candidate will provide technical knowledge, software engineering and leadership ability for:

  • a growing portfolio of data and knowledge management projects - including the ISA software suite and BioSharing registry - funded by UK, EU and NIH Big Data to Knowledge Initiative, and
  • activities for the ELIXIR UK node of the ELIXIR programme.

Job title: Research Software Engineer
Location: University of Oxford, Oxford e-Research Centre, 7 Keble Road, Oxford, OX1 3QG, UK
Contract type: 3 years fixed term, in the first instance (salary range £30-40k, or according to experience)

See here for details on how to apply, link to application form and detailed job description. The deadline for applications is January 19, 2015.
