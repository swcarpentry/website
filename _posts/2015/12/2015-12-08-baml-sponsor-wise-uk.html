This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

We are very happy to announce that the first UK Software Carpentry Workshop for Women in Science and Engineering received generous support from Bank of America Merrill Lynch. The event will take place on 14 and 15th December at the University of Manchester.

We are very grateful for the Bank of America Merrill Lynch support, and look forward to the event!
