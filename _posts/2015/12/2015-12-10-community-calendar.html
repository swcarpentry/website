During this year we host many events for the community including lab meetings, sub-committees and de/briefing sessions. Unfortunately we fail to let everyone know of the events and to try solve this problem next year we are launching a community calendar. If you want to add the community calendar into your agenda you should use https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/oseuuoht0tvjbokgg3noh8c47g%40group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics.
