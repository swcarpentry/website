Raniere Silva has posted a summary of a workshop at the University of Campinas at which Jennifer Shelton, Maneesha Sane, and Natalie Robinson taught over the web. 23 learners from 3 countries enjoyed lessons and pizza, and we learned a few more things about how to teach remotely.
