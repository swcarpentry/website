This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

During last week's Steering Committee meeting we talked about applying to Google Summer of Code as a mentoring organization and decided to contact NumFOCUS to suggest that they applying as a umbrella organization for all the projects they support. NumFOCUS agreed, and for the past few days we have been racing to fill in the application.

We have now completed the online application and we are looking for mentors and projects. Instructions to how became a mentor or suggest a project are available here. If you have questions or feedback, please contact us.
