This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

If you asked to enrol in the Winter 2011 run of the course, you should have received an email request from us on Wednesday pointing you at a form we'd like you to fill in, and another message today clarifying one of the questions. You should also then have received a message saying, "Some people are reporting that they only got #2 of the above, presumably because their spam filters ate #1, so please check." If you didn't get Wednesday's message, or this morning's repeat, please contact us directly and we'll sort it out.
