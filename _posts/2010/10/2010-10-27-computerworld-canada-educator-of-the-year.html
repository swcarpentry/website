This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

ComputerWorld Canada has named Software Carpentry's Greg Wilson the 2010 IT Educator of the Year for "...recognizing the application of innovative techniques and development of new curriculum and delivery of programs that stimulate learning." Greg would like to thank Prof. Karen Reid and Prof. Marsha Chechik for the nomination; the students who have taken or worked on this course; and most especially, his mum and dad, who taught him the things that matter most.
