This post originally appeared on the Software Carpentry website.

I'm pleased to announce that we'll be running Software Carpentry online October-December 2010 for up to 40 graduate students and researchers at Ontario universities. Please see the Fall 2010 page for details (as we get them), or contact us if you would like to enrol. The course is free, but non-credit.
