Yuka Takemon is a research assistant at The Jackson Laboratory working both as a wet lab biologist and a bioinformatic analysis for the Korstanje Lab. Her work mainly focuses on identifying causal and modifier genes for human age-related kidney disease such as Alport Syndrome, using bioinformatic tools to for QTL analysis, GWA studies, and RNA-seq analysis. If not in the lab, she can be found hiking around Acadia National Park, which she considers to be her backyard.
