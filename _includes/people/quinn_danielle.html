Danielle Quinn is a PhD student at Memorial University of Newfoundland in St. John's, Newfoundland, Canada, with an interest in marine ecology and conservation. Her current research focuses on developing novel statistical tools to maximize the utility of data associated with poorly documented or understood species. She has a special interest in encouraging girls and young women to pursue STEM careers and is involved with Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) and Females in the Natural Sciences (FINS) programs.
