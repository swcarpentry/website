Diya Das is finishing her PhD in Molecular & Cell Biology at the University of California, Berkeley, where she is also a Moore/Sloan Data Science Fellow at the Berkeley Institute for Data Science. She studies how stem cells regenerate the olfactory epithelium after severe injury. Diya is involved in various educational efforts, including developing and leading tutorials for The Hacker Within and the CDIPS Data Science Workshop. She is also a co-director of Beyond Academia. You can find Diya online at diyadas.github.io.
