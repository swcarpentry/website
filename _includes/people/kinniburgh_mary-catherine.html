Mary Catherine Kinniburgh is a PhD candidate at The Graduate Center, City University of New York in the English Program. Her work addresses intersections of twentieth-century technologies, poetic movements, and the occult. At her home institution, she teaches digital humanities workshops on topics that range from Arduino to text encoding, and coordinates digital research institutes in collaboration with the Graduate Center Digital Initiatives where she serves as a Digital Fellow. She is also a literary manuscripts specialist at The New York Public Library in the Berg Collection.
