Nihan Pol is a PhD student at West Virginia University. His specialization is Astronomy, specifically neutron stars, pulsars and gravitational waves. Nihan is part of the North American Nanohertz Observatory for Gravitational waves (NANOGrav) which aims to detect nano-hertz frequency gravitational waves using pulsar timing. He makes extensive use of Python in his research and uses it to deal with the large volumes of data found in Astronomy.
