Alexander Konovalov is a Senior Research Fellow in the Centre for Interdisciplinary Research in Computational Algebra and one of the developers of the open-source mathematical software system called GAP. He is involved in multiple aspects of GAP development, technical support and user training, especially through his participation in the CoDiMa and OpenDreamKit projects. He is a Fellow of the Software Sustainability Institute and an editor of the Journal of Software for Algebra and Geometry and the Journal of Open Research Software.
