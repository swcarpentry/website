Michael Connell, Ed.D., holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and a Doctorate in Education from Harvard University. His research has centered mostly on applied cognitive science and evidence-based educational technology design, and for over a decade he has consulted to startups, non-profits, for-profit corporations, and governmental organizations in the areas of instructional design, assessment design, educational technology design, and data science. He is currently Vice President of Training at Enthought, Inc.
