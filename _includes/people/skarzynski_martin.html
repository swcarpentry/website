Martin Skarzynski is a Cancer Prevention Fellow at the National Cancer Institute interested in Genomics, Immunology, and Data Science. In addition to the Carpentries, Martin is an instructor for the Foundation for Advanced Education in Sciences (FAES) Biotech Program and FAES Bioinformatics & Data Science Department. Martin has a PhD in Tumor Biology from Georgetown University, an MS in Biotechnology from Jagiellonian University, and (as of mid-2018) an MPH in Biostatistics & Epidemiology from Johns Hopkins University.
