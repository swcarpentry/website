Deborah Paul has a background which includes a M. S. in Education, and B. S. in Biology. She presently works as a Digitization and Workforce Training Specialist on the iDigBio Project which provides over 83 million specimen records and media for scientific research. The command line became a friend when she needed to rename thousands of image files in hundreds of directories. She enjoys helping to empower anyone interested to learn new biodiversity informatics skills that enhance productivity, reproducible research, and scientific data literacy. In her spare time, she's probably making homemade pizza or apple pie or dancing.
