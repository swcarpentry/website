The Predictive Ecosystem Analyzer (PEcAn) is an integrated ecological bioinformatics toolbox that provides tools to synthesize plant and ecosystem data with mechanistic understanding encoded in ecosystem models. PEcAn is not a model itself, but a platform for model parameterization and calibration that wraps simulation models within a Bayesian framework. The PEcAn Project is an open community of scientists, programmers, and educators and we welcome new contributions and applications.
