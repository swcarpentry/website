Instructors

We run a training course to help people become instructors. The online version requires 2-4 hours/week for 12 weeks, while the live version runs over two full days. The course covers the basics of educational psychology, instructional design, and how to apply both to teaching programming to adults.

Registration for instructor training is currently on hold while we try to clear our backlog. If you have already applied to take part, we will keep your application in queue. Registration will reopen in 2016—please keep an eye on our blog for announcements.

If you are already an instructor and would like to let us know that you have moved, changed your email address, or are able to teach new lessons, please fill in this form to update your information.
