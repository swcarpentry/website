2016 Elections

The election for the 2016 Steering Committee will take place in the week of February 15-19, 2016. The people below have put themselves forward as candidates, and will take part in question and answer sessions on February 9, 2016 to talk to the community about their candidacy and how they hope to contribute to the future of Software Carpentry.

Dhavide Aruliah Jonathan Guyer Rayna Harris Kate Hertweck Karin Lagesen Cam Macdonell Lauren Michael
Bill Mills Giacomo Peru Raniere Silva Leanne Wake Anelda van der Walt Belinda Weaver Jason Williams
