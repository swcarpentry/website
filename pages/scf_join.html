The Software Carpentry Foundation currently offers several tiers of organizational membership:

  1. A Partner is an organization that has made a long-term commitment to the growth and spread of Software Carpentry. Partners are guaranteed one dedicated instructor training event per year, and may appoint a representative to the SCF's Advisory Council.

  2. An Affiliate is an organization that is building a longer-term relationship with the SCF. Affiliates are guaranteed seats in local or online instructor training events, and may also appoint representatives to the Advisory Council.

  3. A Sponsor is an organization that wishes to support SCF activities in a significant way, but does not wish to host workshops. Sponsors are also guaranteed seats in local or online instructor training events.

  4. A Donor is any other individual or organization that supports the SCF's activities financially.

We are always interested in developing new partnerships, and in participating in grants and other proposals. To reach us, or to find out more, please send us email.
